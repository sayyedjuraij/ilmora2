# Ilmora Education - Premium Website Design

## Brand Identity
- **Name**: Ilmora Education
- **Tagline**: Complete Your Graduation Without Leaving Your Job
- **Target**: Working professionals, dropouts, students in UAE & India

## Color Palette
- **Primary Black**: #0a0a0a (Hero, Footer)
- **Rich Gold**: #c9a227 (Accents, CTAs, Highlights)
- **Light Gold**: #e8d5a3 (Hover states)
- **Pure White**: #ffffff (Text on dark, backgrounds)
- **Off White**: #f8f8f8 (Section backgrounds)
- **Dark Gray**: #1a1a1a (Cards)
- **Medium Gray**: #666666 (Body text)
- **Light Gray**: #e5e5e5 (Borders)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Playfair Display (elegant, premium)
- **Body**: Inter (clean, modern)
- **Accent**: Cinzel (luxury feel for labels)

## Section Structure

### 1. Hero Section
- Full viewport height
- Dark background with subtle gradient
- Large headline with gold accent
- Trust badges with checkmarks
- Dual CTA buttons (Eligibility + WhatsApp)
- Floating phone number

### 2. Problem Section (Emotional Hook)
- Light background
- Three pain points with icons
- Relatable messaging
- Transition to solution

### 3. About/Solution Section
- Dark background
- Company introduction
- Key benefits grid
- Statistics counter

### 4. Programs Section
- White background
- Four program categories
- Expandable cards
- Clear descriptions

### 5. Universities Section
- Partner university logos
- Grid layout
- Hover effects
- Clickable links

### 6. Services Section
- Six service cards
- Icon + title + description
- Dark cards on light bg

### 7. Trust Section
- Testimonials slider
- Trust badges
- Student photos
- Rating stars

### 8. How It Works
- 5-step process
- Numbered timeline
- Clear descriptions
- Visual connectors

### 9. Final CTA
- Dark background
- Strong headline
- Multiple contact options
- Urgency elements

### 10. Footer
- Contact info
- Quick links
- Social icons
- Copyright
